Venues

Bandera's Stages: Uniting Venues and Voices

11th Street Cowboy Bar

Known for its rich history and vibrant energy, 11th Street Cowboy Bar is a Bandera landmark that perfectly captures the spirit of Texas. With its large outdoor stage and wide-open space, it’s the ideal venue for the festival’s grand finale, offering room for both performers and an attentive crowd to come together for an unforgettable closing celebration. For decades, this iconic venue has been a gathering spot for locals and visitors alike, known for its authentic Texas honky-tonk vibe and live music scene. Its deep-rooted connection to Bandera’s culture makes it the perfect place to close out the festival, celebrating the town’s musical heritage and community spirit.

Bandera Brewery

Located in the heart of Bandera, Texas, Bandera Brewery offers a cozy, welcoming atmosphere with locally crafted beers and a rustic charm. Its spacious outdoor patio and inviting interior provide the perfect setting for relaxation and connection. With its friendly vibe and central location, it’s the ideal venue for our Friday night VIP party, offering an exclusive opportunity to mingle with songwriters and sponsors while enjoying great music and refreshing brews.

First National Ice Haus

The First National Ice Haus combines rustic charm with a modern touch, making it a unique venue for festival performances. With its open layout and inviting interior, it creates an intimate space for artists to connect with the audience while the cool, historic setting adds to the event’s Texas charm. It's the perfect place for discovering new music and immersing in the festival’s energy.

Western Trail Heritage Park

Located in the heart of Bandera’s Main Street, Heritage Park offers a charming, open-air setting perfect for festival performances. Surrounded by the town’s vibrant atmosphere, the park provides a beautiful backdrop for live music, allowing attendees to enjoy performances while taking in the sights and sounds of downtown. Its central location and relaxed vibe make it an ideal space for creating a memorable, immersive festival experience.

Kickback Korner Bar and Grill

With its classic Texas honky-tonk vibe, Kickback Korner offers an authentic, intimate indoor setting for festival performances. The venue’s rustic charm, combined with its cozy, lively atmosphere, makes it the perfect space for enjoying music up close. The warm, welcoming environment provides an ideal backdrop for songwriters to connect with the audience and create an unforgettable experience.
