VIP Mixer @ Bandera Brewery

The festival kicks off with an exclusive VIP Mixer at Bandera Brewery, offering sponsors, organizers, and attendees the unique chance to mingle with the talented songwriters featured in the festival. In an intimate setting, each songwriter will perform one song of their choice for the eclectic crowd. Sponsors will receive a specific number of tickets based on their sponsorship package, ensuring an unforgettable evening of music and connection.

Official Festival Performances

VIP Breakfast & Song Circle
Start the day with an exclusive VIP Breakfast on the second floor of First National Ice Haus, where special VIP badge holders and songwriters will enjoy breakfast together. This intimate gathering will include a collaborative song circle, allowing the festival's official songwriters to share and connect in a relaxed, creative atmosphere.

Official Festival Performances
The performances begin at 12pm, with three stages—Heritage Park, Kickback Korner, and First National Ice Haus—showcasing incredible talent. From 12pm to 8pm, each stage will feature three songwriters performing in the round style for one-hour sets, offering a dynamic mix of musical styles and stories.

Songwriter Open Mic
After the main performances, Kickback Korner will host a Songwriter Open Mic starting at 9pm. This event will be first come, first served, allowing songwriters to take the stage and share their music in a laid-back, Bandera-style celebration of song.

Grand Finale @ 11th Street Cowboy Bar

The festival wraps up with a grand finale at 11th Street Cowboy Bar, one of Bandera’s most iconic music venues. Starting at 12pm, all participating songwriters will perform one last time in the round style in this legendary setting. This free event offers attendees the chance to interact with the artists, purchase merchandise, and connect with sponsors and festival organizers.
