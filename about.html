About Bandera Songwriters Festival

Celebrating the Heartbeat of Texas Music

Get Involved

Our Mission

The Bandera Songwriters Festival honors the deep-rooted tradition of songwriting that has long been a cornerstone of our town’s cultural identity. Through this event, we aim to preserve and promote the art of songwriting, while inspiring new generations of musicians.

A Celebration of Community

The festival is more than just a musical event; it is a community celebration. Our goal is to connect musicians and fans in an intimate setting that showcases the very best of Bandera’s culture and hospitality.

How You Can Support

The Bandera Songwriters Festival is a (pending) 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We rely on the support of sponsors, volunteers, and the community to make this event possible. Join us in our mission to keep the music alive in Bandera!
