Welcome to the Bandera Songwriters Festival

Celebrating the Heartbeat of Texas Music

Get Involved

Annual Event

The Bandera Songwriters Festival, a (pending) 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, invites you to join us for an unforgettable weekend from April 25-27, 2025, as we celebrate the rich musical heritage of Bandera, Texas. This highly anticipated annual event brings together local, regional, and national songwriters for a vibrant, community-driven celebration of music and storytelling.

Celebration of Community

Held across a variety of charming and intimate venues—First National Ice Haus, Heritage Park, Kickback Korner, Bandera Brewery, and 11th Street Cowboy Bar—the festival offers a unique opportunity to experience live music in the heart of Texas. Each venue provides a distinctive backdrop for performances, allowing festival-goers to explore the dynamic talents of featured songwriters.
